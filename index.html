Introduction

Welcome to The Night House | בית הלילה!

To gain access to the main part of the server, you will need to request the @Members | חברים role from a @Staff | צוות member in the #role-request channel.

In that channel you may also optionally self assign a number of roles, listed out below. The roles that can be self assigned have clickable green buttons which, when pressed, will copy the role assignment command to your system clipboard so that you can easily paste it in the channel.

Please only paste and send one command at a time. The bot does not know how to parse more than one command per message.

Example: n.iam <@&504966239765135360> would assign you to the @Notify | להודיע role.

To unassign a self-assigned role, use the same command but replace n.iam with n.iamn.

Example: n.iamn <@&504966239765135360> would unassign you from the @Notify | להודיע role.

When you get the opportunity, please also leave a quick introduction in the #introduction channel. In addition to learning more about you and your practice, we'd also like to know how you found us!

Staff

@Administrator | כוהנת - this is my admin role, and the Hebrew means "priestess" [cohenet].

@Moderators | מנהלים - this is the role for our most trusted, senior moderators, who must be over the age of 18 (legal majority). The Hebrew means "moderators" [menahelim].

@Assistant Moderators | עוזר מנהלים - this is the role for assistant/junior moderators, who may be under 18 provided that they are sufficiently trustworthy to handle such duties. The Hebrew means "assistant moderators" [ozer menahelim].

@Staff | צוות - this role is for server staff, so when you need help with something you can ping this role and everyone who helps manage the server will get pinged, rather than just one tier of moderators. The Hebrew means "staff" [tzevet].

General

@Members | חברים - this is a general role for server members, and it is required to access the main part of the server. The Hebrew simply means "members" [khaverim].

@Bots | רובוטים - Bots

@Guests | אורחים - this role is auto assigned to server members when they join. It is a temporary role denoting that a user lacks access to the main part of the server, and will be removed and replaced with the Members role once the user demonstrates sufficient initiative / humanity to be admitted to the server (i.e. reading the #rules and #roles, leaving an #introduction, and visiting #role-request to ask for roles. The Hebrew means "guests" [orkhim].

Copy | @Ask To DM | שאל לפני לשלח הודעות - this role is to signify that you might be OK with receiving direct messages, if you are asked first and reply in the affirmative. The Hebrew means "ask before sending messages" [she'el lifney lishloakh hodaot].

Copy | @Do Not DM | לא לשלח הודעות - this role is to signify that you do not consent to receiving direct messages from anyone on this server, but please note that moderators may always message you for important communication, e.g. if you've broken a rule and disciplinary action needs to be taken against you. The Hebrew means "do not send messages" [lo lishloakh hodaot].
